<p><p>The fingerprint lock market can be categorized into two main types: fingerprint-only unlocking and multiple method unlocking. Fingerprint-only unlocking utilizes biometric technology, allowing access solely through fingerprint recognition, offering convenience and security. In contrast, multiple method unlocking combines fingerprint scanning with additional methods like PINs, facial recognition, or keys, providing users with enhanced flexibility and security options. This dual approach caters to varying user preferences, balancing ease of use with varying levels of secure access control.</p></p>
