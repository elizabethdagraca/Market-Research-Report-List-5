<p><p>The industrial rectifiers market can be segmented into three main types: Low Current Rectifiers (LCR), Medium Current Rectifiers (MCR), and High Current Rectifiers (HCR). LCRs are designed for applications requiring low power levels and are widely used in smaller electronic devices. MCRs cater to medium-level industrial applications, offering a balance of efficiency and power. HCRs, on the other hand, are suited for high-power applications, such as electrolysis and heavy metal plating, demanding robust performance and reliability.</p></p>
