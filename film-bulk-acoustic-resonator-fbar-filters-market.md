<p><p>Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator (FBAR) filters are categorized primarily into ladder type and lattice type, among others. Ladder type filters utilize a series of connected resonators to achieve desired frequency selectivity, making them ideal for applications requiring sharp filtering. Lattice type filters, on the other hand, employ a grid arrangement to enhance bandwidth and minimize insertion loss, offering greater versatility. Other market types may include customized designs or hybrid configurations, tailored for specific applications in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and other sectors.</p></p>
