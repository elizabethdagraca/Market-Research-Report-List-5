<p><strong>The Optical Plastic Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Lens</li><li>Aspherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical plastic lens market comprises two main types: spherical and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses have a consistent curvature, offering simple manufacturing and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in eyewear and basic optical applications. In contrast, aspherical lenses feature varying curvatures across their surface, enabling improved image quality, reduced distortion, and a slimmer profile. This allows for enhanced performance in high-end applications like cameras, binoculars, and advanced eyewear, driving demand for aspherical lenses despite their higher production complexity and costs.</p></p>
