<p><p>The chemical sunscreen market is categorized primarily into UVA and UVB sunscreen segments. UVA sunscreens protect the skin against long-wave ultraviolet rays, which can lead to premature aging and skin cancer. They often contain active ingredients like avobenzone and octocrylene. UVB sunscreens, on the other hand, guard against short-wave rays responsible for sunburn and skin damage, featuring ingredients such as octinoxate and homosalate. Both types are essential for comprehensive sun protection, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
