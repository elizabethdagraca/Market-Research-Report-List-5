<p><strong>The Fiberoptic Blanket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Knitting Fiberoptic Blanket</li><li>Woven Fiberoptic Blanket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberoptic blanket market is primarily segmented into two types: knitting fiberoptic blankets and woven fiberoptic blankets. Knitting fiberoptic blankets utilize interconnected loops of yarn, providing flexibility and enhanced light distribution, making them suitable for a variety of applications. In contrast, woven fiberoptic blankets are crafted through interlacing threads, offering durability and structural integrity. Both types are employed in various sectors, including healthcare, textiles, and insulation, to leverage their unique properties for thermal management and illumination purposes.</p></p>
