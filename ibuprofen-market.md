<p><p>The Ibuprofen market is divided into two main types based on standards: USP (United States Pharmacopeia) and EP (European Pharmacopoeia). USP Ibuprofen complies with standards set by the United States Pharmacopeia, ensuring quality, purity, and potency for the American market. In contrast, EP Ibuprofen adheres to guidelines established by the European Pharmacopoeia, focusing on similar quality parameters for the European market. These distinctions influence regulatory approvals, manufacturing practices, and marketing strategies tailored to their respective regions.</p></p>
