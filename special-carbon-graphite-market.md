<p><p>The special carbon graphite market encompasses various types, including isotropic graphite, extruded graphite, molded graphite, and others. Isotropic graphite exhibits uniform properties in all directions, making it ideal for applications requiring consistent performance. Extruded graphite is produced by forcing carbon material through a die, resulting in high density and strength, suited for various industrial uses. Molded graphite is shaped under pressure, offering excellent thermal and electrical conductivity. Other graphite forms include flexible and fine-grained varieties, catering to specialized applications in diverse industries.</p></p>
