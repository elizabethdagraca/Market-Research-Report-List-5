<p><p>In the oil and gas market, digital twins are classified into three types: Product Digital Twin, Process Digital Twin, and System Digital Twin. Product Digital Twins represent the lifecycle of physical assets, optimizing design and performance. Process Digital Twins simulate and enhance operational workflows, improving efficiency and safety. System Digital Twins model entire operations, integrating multiple processes and assets to provide a comprehensive view, facilitating strategic decision-making and predictive maintenance across oil and gas operations.</p></p>
