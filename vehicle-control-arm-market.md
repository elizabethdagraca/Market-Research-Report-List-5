Stamped steel control arms are lightweight and cost-effective, providing good durability for a variety of vehicles. Cast iron control arms offer enhanced strength and stiffness, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications but are heavier. Cast aluminum control arms are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, improving vehicle performance and fuel efficiency. Each type serves specific vehicle needs based on weight, cost, and performance requirements.</p></p>
