<p><p>The automatic stretch wrap machine market consists of three primary types: Rotary stretch wrap machines use a rotating arm to wrap products on a stationary platform, ensuring uniform coverage. Turntable stretch wrap machines feature a rotating table where products are placed and wrapped as they spin, offering efficiency for various load sizes. Robotic stretch wrap machines utilize automated robotic arms to wrap products, providing flexibility and mobility, ideal for adjustable or uneven loads. Each type caters to specific operational needs and production capacities.</p></p>
