<p><p>The AC-DC power conversion market encompasses two primary types: external AC-DC power supplies and embedded AC-DC power solutions. External AC-DC power supplies are standalone units that convert alternating current (AC) from wall outlets into direct current (DC) for various devices. These are commonly used in consumer electronics. Conversely, embedded AC-DC power solutions are integrated within appliances or systems, providing efficient power conversion without the need for external adapters. Both types cater to diverse applications, emphasizing efficiency and reliability in power management.</p></p>
