<p><ul><li>Electrochemiluminescence</li><li>Colloidal Gold</li><li>Chemiluminescence</li><li>Isotope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunodiagnostic reagent market encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Electrochemiluminescence** utilizes an electrical signal to produce light for detection, offering high sensitivity and specificity. </p><p>2. **Colloidal Gold** employs nanoparticles to visualize antigen-antibody interactions, commonly used in rapid tests due to its simplicity. </p><p>3. **Chemiluminescence** relies on chemical reactions to emit light for measuring analytes, valued for its accuracy. </p><p>4. **Isotope-based reagents** use radioactive substances to trace and quantify biological markers, mainly in research and specialized diagnostics.</p></p>
