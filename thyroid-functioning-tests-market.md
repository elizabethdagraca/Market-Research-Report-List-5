FT3 and FT4 tests measure the levels of free triiodothyronine and thyroxine, respectively, evaluating the active hormones produced by the thyroid gland. These tests are essential for diagnosing thyroid disorders, monitoring treatment, and guiding clinical decisions, contributing to a significant market segment driven by rising awareness and prevalence of thyroid conditions.</p></p>
