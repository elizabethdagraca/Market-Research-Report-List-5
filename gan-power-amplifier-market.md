<p><p>The GaN power amplifier market comprises two primary types: GaN-on-SiC and GaN-on-Silicon. GaN-on-SiC technology utilizes silicon carbide as a substrate, offering superior thermal conductivity and efficiency, making it suitable for high-power applications like telecommunications and military systems. In contrast, GaN-on-Silicon leverages conventional silicon wafers, providing cost-effective solutions with good performance for lower power applications, such as consumer electronics and emerging 5G networks. Each type caters to distinct application requirements, balancing performance and cost.</p></p>
