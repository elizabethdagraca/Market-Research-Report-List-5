<p><p>Ear thermometers are categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact. Non-contact ear thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, providing quick and hygienic readings without skin contact. In contrast, contact type ear thermometers require placement inside the ear canal, using a probe to measure temperature directly. While non-contact thermometers are favored for their convenience and speed, contact thermometers offer precise measurements, making both types valuable in clinical and home healthcare settings.</p></p>
