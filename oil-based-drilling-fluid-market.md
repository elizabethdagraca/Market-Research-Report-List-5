<p><p>The oil-based drilling fluid market can be categorized into two primary types: coarse drilling fluid and high-quality drilling fluid. Coarse drilling fluid typically contains larger particles and is suited for less complex drilling environments, providing adequate lubrication and stability. In contrast, high-quality drilling fluid is formulated with finer additives, offering superior properties such as enhanced viscosity, improved stability, and better filtration control. This type is ideal for challenging drilling conditions, ensuring optimal performance and reduced downtime in deep or complex wells.</p></p>
