<p><p>Pipe thread tape, also known as Teflon tape, is a non-adhesive tape used to seal the threads of pipes and fittings. <p><p>The pipe thread tape market features various types, including standard density, high density, full density, and liquid tape. Standard density tape is ideal for general sealing needs, while high density offers superior strength and durability for demanding applications. Full density tape provides the highest density for heavy-duty requirements, ensuring leak-free seals under pressure. Liquid tape, on the other hand, functions as a sealant that can be brushed or sprayed onto threads, creating a flexible and waterproof seal, suitable for diverse environments.</p></p>
