<p><p>The conveyor belt market comprises several key types, including multiply fabric conveyor belts, steel cord conveyor belts, and solid woven conveyor belts. Multiply fabric belts are made from several layers of fabric, offering flexibility and strength for various applications. Steel cord belts incorporate high-strength steel cables, enabling them to transport heavy loads over long distances and harsh conditions. Solid woven conveyor belts are designed for high durability and provide resistance against abrasion, making them suitable for mining and heavy-duty industries.</p></p>
