<p><p>The light-transmitting concrete market is primarily categorized into two types: fiber light-transmitting concrete and resin light-transmitting concrete. Fiber light-transmitting concrete incorporates optical fibers that enable light to travel through the material, creating unique visual effects and enhancing architectural designs. In contrast, resin light-transmitting concrete utilizes a resin base to impart light-transmitting properties, offering increased durability and versatility. Both types are popular in modern construction for applications such as decorative facades, artistic installations, and illuminated surfaces.</p></p>
