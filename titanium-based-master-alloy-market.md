<p><p>The Titanium-Based Master Alloy market is segmented into two main types: primary and secondary titanium-based master alloys. Primary titanium-based master alloys are produced directly from titanium ore and typically contain high titanium content, serving as raw materials for alloy production. In contrast, secondary titanium-based master alloys are derived from recycling titanium scrap and other titanium-containing materials, offering a more sustainable option. Both types play crucial roles in manufacturing high-performance titanium alloys for industries like aerospace, automotive, and medical applications.</p></p>
