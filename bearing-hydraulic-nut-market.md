Shim Type Nuts are designed for precise clamping by allowing the adjustment of preload through shims. Lower Collar Nuts provide stability and load distribution, ensuring secure assembly in applications. Upper Collar Nuts, positioned above the bearing, enhance load capacity and resist axial movement. Each type serves specific functions in mechanical assemblies, contributing to optimal performance and durability in industrial applications.</p></p>
