<p><p>Card access control readers are essential for secure entry systems, comprising two main market types: keyboard and contactless. Keyboard readers require users to input a personal identification number (PIN) alongside their card, enhancing security through dual authentication. In contrast, contactless readers utilize RFID technology, allowing users to gain access by simply waving their card near the reader, offering convenience and speed. Both types serve distinct needs in security and ease, catering to various environments and user preferences.</p></p>
