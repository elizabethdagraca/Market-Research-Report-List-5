<p><p>The personal cleaning product ingredients market is categorized into three main types: anionic cleaners, non-ionic cleaners, and amphoteric cleaners. Anionic cleaners are negatively charged and are effective in removing dirt and grease, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Non-ionic cleaners lack a charge, providing gentle cleaning without excessive foam, ideal for sensitive skin products. Amphoteric cleaners possess both positive and negative charges, allowing them to adapt to different pH levels and surfaces, making them versatile for various cleaning purposes.</p></p>
