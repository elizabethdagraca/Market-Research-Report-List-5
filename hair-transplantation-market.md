<p><strong>The Hair Transplantation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair transplantation market primarily consists of two types: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves removing a strip of scalp tissue to harvest hair follicles, which are then implanted in balding areas, resulting in a linear scar. In contrast, FUE extracts individual hair follicles directly from the scalp using specialized tools, leaving minimal scarring and allowing for quicker recovery. Both methods cater to different patient preferences and hair restoration needs, driving growth in the market.</p></p>
