Cockpit Voice Recorders (CVR) capture audio conversations in the cockpit, providing insights into crew interactions and decision-making processes during flight. Combined Voice and Flight Data Recorders (CVFDR) integrate both functionalities, allowing for comprehensive analysis of both technical and human factors during military operations and incidents.</p></p>
