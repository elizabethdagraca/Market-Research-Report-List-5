<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The attitude gyroscope market is categorized into two main types: analog and digital. Analog gyroscopes use mechanical or electromechanical systems to measure rotational motion, providing continuous output via analog signals. In contrast, digital gyroscopes leverage electronic sensors and microprocessors to convert rotational data into digital signals, offering higher precision and integration capabilities. The choice between these types often depends on specific application needs, such as cost, size, weight, and accuracy, influencing their adoption across various industries like aerospace, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
