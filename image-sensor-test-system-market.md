<p><p>The Image Sensor Test System market encompasses three primary types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), and CIS (Contact Image Sensor). CMOS sensors are widely used in consumer electronics, offering high-speed performance and lower power consumption. CCD sensors are known for superior image quality and are commonly found in professional cameras. CIS, often utilized in scanners and barcode readers, converts optical images into electronic signals. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing testing methods and system requirements.</p></p>
