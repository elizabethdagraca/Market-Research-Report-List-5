<p><p>The Vehicle Positioning Module market is segmented into centimeter-level and decimeter-level positioning. Centimeter-level positioning provides highly precise location data, crucial for applications such as autonomous driving, precision agriculture, and advanced mapping. It ensures accuracy within a few centimeters, enabling reliable navigation and decision-making. In contrast, decimeter-level positioning offers slightly less precision but is suitable for applications like fleet management, logistics, and road safety enhancements. This level still allows for efficient route planning and vehicle tracking while being more cost-effective.</p></p>
