<p><p>The 3D Time-of-Flight (ToF) camera market primarily includes CMOS and CCD technologies. CMOS ToF cameras leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor sensors, offering benefits like lower power consumption and faster readout speeds, making them suitable for mobile devices and real-time applications. On the other hand, CCD ToF cameras utilize charge-coupled device sensors, known for their high-quality imaging and sensitivity, ideal for industrial and professional settings. Both technologies cater to diverse applications, enhancing depth perception and spatial detection.</p></p>
