<p><p>The industrial machinery brakes market is primarily segmented into drum brakes and disc brakes. Drum brakes consist of a cylindrical drum that houses brake shoes, providing strong stopping power and durability, often used in heavy machinery for robust applications. On the other hand, disc brakes feature a rotating disc and caliper, offering consistent performance and quick heat dissipation, making them ideal for precision applications. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety, control, and efficiency in various industrial processes.</p></p>
