<p><strong>The Automotive Seatbelt Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buckle Pretensioner</li><li>Retractor Pretensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seatbelt pretensioner market comprises two primary types: buckle pretensioners and retractor pretensioners. Buckle pretensioners are designed to tighten the seatbelt at the buckle when a collision is detected, reducing slack and enhancing occupant restraint. In contrast, retractor pretensioners function by retracting the seatbelt webbing during an impact, ensuring a snug fit against the occupant. Both types play a crucial role in improving passenger safety by minimizing movement during a crash and maximizing the effectiveness of seatbelt systems.</p></p>
