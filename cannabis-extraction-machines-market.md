<p><p>Cannabis extraction machines are categorized into several types based on the extraction method used. Supercritical CO2 extraction machines utilize carbon dioxide under high pressure to isolate cannabinoids and terpenes efficiently. Ethanol extraction machines employ ethanol as a solvent, ideal for large-scale production. Hydrocarbon extraction machines use hydrocarbons like butane for high-quality concentrates. Solvent-less extraction machines utilize physical methods like heat and pressure to extract compounds without chemicals. Additionally, "Others" encompasses emerging technologies and hybrid methods enhancing extraction efficiency.</p></p>
