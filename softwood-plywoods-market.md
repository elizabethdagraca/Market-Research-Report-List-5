<p><p>Softwood plywood is categorized by thickness into three primary market types: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywood below 6mm typically serves applications like interior paneling and cabinetry, where lightweight materials are required. The 6mm-18mm range is versatile, commonly used in furniture, flooring, and construction, balancing strength and weight. Plywood exceeding 18mm is ideal for structural applications, providing enhanced durability and load-bearing capacity, essential in heavy-duty construction and industrial settings. Each category caters to specific customer needs and market demands.</p></p>
