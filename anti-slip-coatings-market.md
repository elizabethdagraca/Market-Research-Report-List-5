<p><p>The anti-slip coatings market is primarily divided into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions, quick drying times, and ease of application, making them ideal for indoor environments. In contrast, solvent-based coatings use organic solvents, providing superior durability and chemical resistance, suitable for heavy-duty applications in industrial and outdoor settings. Both types enhance safety by improving traction on various surfaces, catering to diverse needs across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
