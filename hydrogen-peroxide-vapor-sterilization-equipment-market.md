<p><p>The hydrogen peroxide vapor sterilization equipment market consists primarily of two types: chamber decontamination and room decontamination. Chamber decontamination involves sterilizing specific enclosed spaces, such as medical instruments or equipment, ensuring a high level of microbial reduction within a confined area. Conversely, room decontamination targets larger spaces, such as operating rooms or patient care areas, utilizing vaporized hydrogen peroxide to eliminate pathogens throughout the entire room. Both methods are effective in maintaining aseptic conditions in healthcare settings.</p></p>
