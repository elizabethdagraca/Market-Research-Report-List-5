<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mineral Insulated Metal Sheathed (MIMS) thermocouples market is categorized into three main types: grounded, ungrounded, and exposed types. Grounded types feature a direct electrical connection to the sheath, allowing rapid temperature response but potentially introducing noise. Ungrounded types have an insulated junction, minimizing interference but responding more slowly. Exposed types are designed for direct contact with the measured medium, offering instantaneous readings but less protection against environmental influences. Each type serves specific applications based on temperature sensitivity and environmental requirements.</p></p>
