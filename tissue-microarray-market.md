<p><p>The tissue microarray market comprises several types, each serving distinct research and diagnostic purposes. Immunohistochemistry involves the use of antibodies to detect specific proteins in tissue samples, facilitating cancer research and pathology. Fluorescent in-situ hybridization allows for the visualization of genetic material within cells, aiding in genetic studies and cancer diagnostics. Frozen tissue arrays preserve samples for immediate analysis, while "Others" includes emerging techniques and applications, expanding the market's versatility and utility in biomedical research.</p></p>
