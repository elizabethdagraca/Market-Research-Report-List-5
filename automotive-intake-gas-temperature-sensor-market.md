Thermocouple types function effectively over a wide temperature range, ideal for high-heat applications. Semiconductor-based sensors provide compact designs with excellent precision for modern vehicles. Other types may include infrared or optical sensors, catering to specific needs in different automotive applications, contributing to accurate engine performance and emissions control.</p></p>
