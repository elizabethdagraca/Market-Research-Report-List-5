<p><strong>The Carbon Wovens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>General Weave</li><li>Twill Weave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon wovens market is primarily divided into two main types: general weave and twill weave. General weave fabric features a simple over-and-under pattern, providing uniform strength and stability, ideal for various applications. In contrast, twill weave fabric displays a diagonal pattern, enhancing flexibility and drape while maintaining durability. These characteristics make twill weave suitable for applications requiring complex shapes and contours, while general weave is often preferred for structural components in aerospace, automotive, and sports industries.</p></p>
