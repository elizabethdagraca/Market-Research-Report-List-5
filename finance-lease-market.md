<p><p>A finance lease is a type of leasing arrangement where the lessor provides an asset to the lessee for a specified period, allowing the lessee to use the asset while making regular payments. Unlike operating leases, finance leases transfer most of the risks and rewards associated with ownership to the lessee, making them responsible for maintenance and insurance. This arrangement is particularly advantageous for businesses seeking to acquire equipment or vehicles without a large upfront capital investment.</p><p>The finance lease market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for equipment financing across various industries. This growth can be attributed to businesses seeking to preserve cash flow while still accessing essential assets. Furthermore, advancements in technology and the rise of digital platforms facilitating lease transactions are enhancing market accessibility.
