<p><p>The automotive load sensor market includes various types such as wheel spoke sensors, torque transducers, hole sensors, and s-sensors. Wheel spoke sensors are integrated into the wheel assembly to monitor load and pressure changes. Torque transducers measure the torque applied in driveline components, enhancing performance and safety. Hole sensors detect weight distribution and load positions in various parts. S-sensors are specialized sensors designed for precise load measurement in challenging environments, improving overall vehicle dynamics and safety.</p></p>
