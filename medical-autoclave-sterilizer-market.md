<p><p>The medical autoclave sterilizer market is primarily segmented into horizontal and vertical autoclave sterilizers. Horizontal autoclaves feature a cylindrical chamber that opens on the side, making them ideal for sterilizing larger items and bulk loads. They provide efficient steam penetration and are commonly used in laboratories and hospitals. In contrast, vertical autoclaves have a chamber that opens from the top, suitable for smaller instruments and lab glassware. Both types ensure high-temperature steam sterilization for effective disinfection in healthcare settings.</p></p>
