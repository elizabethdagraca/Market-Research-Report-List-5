<p><p>Thin wafer temporary bonding equipment and materials are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, allowing delicate wafers to be processed without damage. There are four main debonding methods: </p><p>1. **Chemical Debonding** utilizes solvents to dissolve bonding agents.</p><p>2. **Hot Sliding Debonding** employs heat and friction to separate layers.</p><p>3. **Mechanical Debonding** involves physical force to detach bonded wafers.</p><p>4. **Laser Debonding** uses focused laser energy to break the bond. Each method offers distinct advantages depending on the application requirements and materials involved.</p></p>
