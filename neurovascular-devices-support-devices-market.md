<p><p>Neurovascular device support devices play a crucial role in minimally invasive neurovascular procedures. Microcatheters are specialized thin tubes designed to deliver treatments or access specific areas within the vascular system of the brain. They enable precise navigation and targeting for procedures like embolization or stent placement. Microguidewires are slender wires used alongside microcatheters to provide a guiding pathway through complex vasculature, enhancing maneuverability and stability during interventions. Together, they improve the effectiveness and safety of neurovascular treatments.</p></p>
