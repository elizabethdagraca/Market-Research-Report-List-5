<p><p>The oil boiler market comprises two primary types: internal oil boilers and external oil boilers. Internal oil boilers are compact units installed within the building, providing efficient heating and space-saving benefits. They are ideal for residential applications where space is limited. Conversely, external oil boilers are standalone units located outside the building, offering easy maintenance and reduced noise levels indoors. These boilers are often used in larger installations or rural settings, where connecting to gas supplies is not feasible.</p></p>
