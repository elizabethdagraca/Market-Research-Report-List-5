<p><p>The Razor Wire market features various types, prominently including Concertina Razor Wire and Flat Razor Wire. Concertina Razor Wire is designed in spiral coils, making it ideal for securing large perimeters by creating an impassable barrier with sharp edges. In contrast, Flat Razor Wire consists of flat strips with barbed edges, offering a streamlined appearance and enhancing security while being easier to handle. Both types are used extensively in security applications for prisons, military facilities, and commercial properties, ensuring effective deterrence.</p></p>
