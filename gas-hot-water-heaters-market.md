<p><ul><li>Tank Storage</li><li>Tankless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas hot water heater market is primarily divided into two types: tank storage and tankless systems. Tank storage heaters feature an insulated tank that stores a significant volume of heated water for immediate use, providing a steady supply but with limited renewable capacity. In contrast, tankless systems heat water on demand, offering unlimited hot water without the bulk of a storage tank, which enhances energy efficiency and reduces space requirements. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for convenience and energy savings.</p></p>
