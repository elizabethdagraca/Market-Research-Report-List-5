<p><p>Handheld conductivity meters are categorized into single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel types based on their measurement capabilities. Single channel meters measure conductivity in one sample at a time, making them suitable for basic applications. Dual channel meters can analyze two samples simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and allowing for comparative analysis. Multi-channel meters offer the flexibility to measure several samples at once, catering to complex scenarios and demanding applications like research labs and industrial processes, where multiple readings are essential.</p></p>
