<p><p>The epitaxial reactor market is primarily categorized into Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD), Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE), and other techniques such as Vapour Phase Epitaxy (VPE), Liquid Phase Epitaxy (LPE), and Solid Phase Epitaxy (SPE). MOCVD is widely used for producing compound semiconductors, while MBE offers precise control over layer thickness and composition. VPE focuses on gas phase reactions to create thin films, LPE involves crystal growth from a liquid solution, and SPE relies on solid-state transformations to achieve epitaxial layers.</p></p>
