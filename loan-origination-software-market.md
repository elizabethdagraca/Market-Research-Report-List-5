<p><p>Loan origination software (LOS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based LOS offers flexibility and scalability, allowing lenders to access the software remotely, facilitating real-time collaboration and easier updates. This model generally reduces IT costs. Conversely, on-premise LOS is installed locally on a lender's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and organizational preferences.</p></p>
