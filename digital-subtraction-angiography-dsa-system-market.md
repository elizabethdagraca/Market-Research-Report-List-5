<p><p>Digital Subtraction Angiography (DSA) is an advanced imaging technique used primarily in vascular studies to visualize blood vessels. It employs a digital process to subtract pre-contrast images from post-contrast images, enhancing the visibility of blood vessels while minimizing surrounding structures. This method is instrumental in diagnosing and guiding treatments for various conditions such as arterial blockages, aneurysms, and congenital vascular abnormalities.</p><p>The DSA System Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing incidences of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in imaging technology, and a growing elderly population requiring diagnostic imaging.
