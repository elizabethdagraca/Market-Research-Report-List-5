<p><p>The high-voltage grounding switch market is categorized into two main types: independent earthing switches and earthing switches with combined current transformers. Independent earthing switches function separately to safely ground electrical equipment, ensuring safety during maintenance. In contrast, earthing switches with combined current transformers integrate grounding and current measurement, enhancing operational efficiency by providing real-time monitoring of electrical currents while maintaining equipment safety. Both types are essential for safeguarding high-voltage systems and ensuring reliable operation in power generation and distribution.</p></p>
