Short fiber composites, on the other hand, consist of shorter glass fibers, offering improved processability and cost-effectiveness, suited for less rigorous applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, leveraging their unique properties for specific performance needs.</p></p>
