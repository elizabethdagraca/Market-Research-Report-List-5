<p><p>The dental orthotic device market comprises several types catering to specific orthodontic needs. Anterior repositioning dental orthotic devices focus on correcting the alignment of the front teeth and jaw, promoting proper occlusion. Anterior bite plane dental orthotic devices are designed to support the anterior jaw position, reducing strain on muscles and joints. Posterior bite plane dental orthotic devices stabilize the back teeth, preventing excessive movement and discomfort. Other devices may include customized solutions for unique dental conditions, further enhancing treatment approaches.</p></p>
