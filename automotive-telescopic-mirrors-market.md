<p><p>Automotive telescopic mirrors are adjustable mirrors designed to enhance visibility and safety. **Door-mounted telescopic mirrors** are attached to the vehicle's doors, offering flexibility and ease of adjustment for drivers. They are commonly used in passenger vehicles and light trucks. **Body-mounted telescopic mirrors** are fixed to the vehicle's body, typically near the fenders or hood, providing a wider field of view. These are often found in heavy-duty trucks and commercial vehicles, offering durability and stability for long-haul or off-road use. Both types cater to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
