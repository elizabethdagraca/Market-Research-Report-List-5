<p><ul><li>Unitised Type</li><li>Stick Built Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction glass curtain wall market primarily consists of two types: unitised and stick-built systems. Unitised curtain walls are pre-fabricated panels, assembled in a factory and installed on-site, offering rapid installation and consistent quality. In contrast, stick-built curtain walls are constructed on-site by assembling individual components, allowing for flexibility in design but often requiring more time and labor. Both types provide aesthetic appeal, energy efficiency, and protection against environmental elements, catering to diverse architectural needs.</p></p>
