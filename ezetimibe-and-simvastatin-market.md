<p><p>Ezetimibe and Simvastatin are lipid-lowering medications used primarily to manage cholesterol levels in patients with hypercholesterolemia. Together, they provide a synergistic effect, effectively lowering low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol levels and reducing cardiovascular risks.</p><p>The Ezetimibe and Simvastatin Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, rising awareness of cholesterol management, and a growing aging population. Advances in pharmaceutical formulations and the introduction of generic versions have also made these medications more accessible.</p><p>Furthermore, healthcare infrastructure improvements and rising disposable incomes in emerging markets are expected to fuel market expansion.
