<p><ul><li>Hard CMP Pads</li><li>Soft CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP polishing pad market is segmented into hard and soft pads, each tailored for specific semiconductor manufacturing applications. Hard CMP pads are designed for aggressive material removal and provide excellent planarity, making them suitable for polishing robust materials like silicon wafers. In contrast, soft CMP pads are engineered for delicate materials, offering better conformability and reduced surface damage. The choice between hard and soft pads depends on the specific requirements of the wafer's electrical properties and desired surface finish.</p></p>
