<p><p>The Wall Keypad Market consists of two primary types: Touch Type and Push Button Type. Touch Type keypads utilize capacitive or resistive technology, offering a seamless and modern interface that responds to finger touches. They often feature customizable backlighting and are favored for their sleek design. In contrast, Push Button Type keypads rely on physical buttons, providing tactile feedback and reliability. These are preferred in environments where durability and ease of use are paramount, such as industrial settings and secure access points.</p></p>
