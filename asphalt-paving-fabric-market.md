<p><ul><li>Woven Fibres</li><li>Non-Woven Fibres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt paving fabric market comprises two main types: woven and non-woven fibers. Woven fibers consist of interlaced strands, providing enhanced tensile strength, making them ideal for applications where durability and load-bearing capacity are crucial. In contrast, non-woven fibers are produced by bonding fibers together, allowing for excellent water and air permeability, which aids in stress relief and moisture management in pavements. Both types play vital roles in improving the longevity and performance of asphalt surfaces in construction projects.</p></p>
