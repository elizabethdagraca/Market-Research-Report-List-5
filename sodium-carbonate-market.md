<p><p>The sodium carbonate market is primarily categorized into light and dense sodium carbonate types. Light sodium carbonate, characterized by its low density and high solubility, is commonly used in applications like glass manufacturing, detergents, and water treatment. In contrast, dense sodium carbonate has a higher bulk density, making it suitable for processes requiring reduced dust formation, such as in metallurgy and chemical production. Both types are essential in various industrial sectors, addressing specific needs and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
