<p><p>The basalt fiber products market primarily consists of two types: basalt chopped fiber and basalt continuous fiber. Basalt chopped fiber is produced by cutting basalt fibers into shorter lengths, making it suitable for reinforcing composite materials, notably in construction and automotive applications. In contrast, basalt continuous fiber is delivered in long strands, ideal for applications requiring high strength and durability, such as in aerospace, marine, and industrial sectors. Both types benefit from basalt's superior thermal resistance and mechanical properties.</p></p>
