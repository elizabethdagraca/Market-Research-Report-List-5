<p><p>The sensor ICs market encompasses various types, including Linear Sensor ICs and Angle Sensor ICs. Linear Sensor ICs are designed to measure linear displacements or quantities, typically providing analog output that corresponds to the input variable. In contrast, Angle Sensor ICs measure rotational angles and often utilize technologies like magnetics or capacitive sensing for precise measurements. Both types are essential across industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation, driving innovation and improving system performance and accuracy.</p></p>
