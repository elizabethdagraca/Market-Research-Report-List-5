<p><p>Hoist rings are essential lifting devices categorized mainly into two market types: center-pull and side-pull. Center-pull hoist rings are designed for vertical lifting, allowing loads to be lifted directly downward, ensuring stability and balance. In contrast, side-pull hoist rings facilitate horizontal lifting applications, enabling loads to be pulled from the side, which is ideal for specific configurations and accessibility. Understanding these distinctions helps in selecting the appropriate hoist ring for various lifting and rigging applications.</p></p>
