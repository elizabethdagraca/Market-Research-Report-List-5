<p><p>The bulletproof jacket market encompasses three main types: soft vests, hard vests, and composite vests. Soft vests are lightweight and flexible, providing comfort for prolonged wear, typically utilizing layers of ballistic fabric. Hard vests incorporate rigid plates to enhance protection against high-caliber threats, ensuring durability in hostile environments. Composite vests combine features of both soft and hard vests, integrating advanced materials for improved security while maintaining a degree of flexibility, catering to various operational needs and threat levels.</p></p>
