<p><strong>The Automotive Pyrotechnic Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buckle Pretensioner</li><li>Retractor Pretensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive pyrotechnic pretensioner market is primarily categorized into two types: buckle pretensioners and retractor pretensioners. Buckle pretensioners are installed in seatbelt buckles, tightening the belt upon collision to secure the occupant better. Retractor pretensioners, on the other hand, are integrated into the seatbelt retractor mechanism, rapidly pulling the belt tight during an impact. Both types enhance occupant safety by reducing slack in the seatbelt, thereby minimizing injury potential during a crash.</p></p>
