<p><p>The silicon-based anode material market consists primarily of two types: SiO/C (Silicon Oxide/Carbon) and Si/C (Silicon/Carbon). SiO/C combines silicon oxide with carbon to enhance cycle stability and improve performance in lithium-ion batteries, catering to a demand for higher energy density and longevity. On the other hand, Si/C uses silicon blended with carbon to achieve significant capacity improvements. Both materials are pivotal in advancing battery technology, appealing to sectors focused on electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
