<p><strong>The Implantable Pulse Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Multipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Implantable Pulse Generator (IPG) market is categorized into two primary types: unipolar and multipolar. Unipolar IPGs use a single electrode for stimulation, allowing for simpler design and easier implantation, but may have limitations in the precision of stimulation. Multipolar IPGs, on the other hand, utilize multiple electrodes, offering more targeted and adjustable stimulation patterns. This enhances therapeutic effectiveness, making multipolar devices increasingly preferred in various medical applications, including pain management and neuromodulation therapies.</p></p>
