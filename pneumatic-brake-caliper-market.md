<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pneumatic Brake Caliper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pneumatic Brake Caliper</li><li>Floating Pneumatic Brake Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Fixed Pneumatic Brake Caliper** is rigidly mounted and uses pistons on both sides of the rotor to apply braking force, offering high performance and stability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. The **Floating Pneumatic Brake Caliper** moves laterally, using a single piston to press the inner brake pad, which then pulls the outer pad, making it compact and cost-effective, suitable for lighter applications. Both types utilize compressed air for actuation, ensuring reliable braking in industrial and automotive systems.</p></p>
