<p><ul><li>Cast Iron Check Valves</li><li>Cast Iron Globe Valves</li><li>Cast Iron Gate Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cast iron valves market comprises three main types: check valves, globe valves, and gate valves. Cast iron check valves prevent backflow, ensuring fluid flows in one direction, ideal for various applications. Globe valves regulate flow with precision, making them suitable for throttling services. Gate valves provide full flow when fully opened, offering minimal resistance, commonly used in water supply and drainage systems. Each valve type serves distinct operational needs, contributing to efficiency and safety in fluid management.</p></p>
