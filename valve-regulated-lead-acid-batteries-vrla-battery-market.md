<p><p>Valve Regulated Lead Acid Batteries (VRLA batteries) are a type of lead-acid battery that incorporates a valve system to recombine gases generated during charging. This design makes them maintenance-free and prevents electrolyte spillage, enhancing safety and versatility in various applications.
