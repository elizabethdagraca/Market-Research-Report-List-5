<p><p>The plastic check valves market comprises various types based on connection methods, primarily focusing on insert connection and threaded construction. Insert connection valves are designed for direct installation into a piping system, offering quick and secure fittings, often used in tight spaces. Threaded construction valves, on the other hand, feature threaded ends for easy attachment to existing pipe systems, ensuring leak-free connections. Both types are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for diverse applications, enhancing efficiency in fluid control systems.</p></p>
