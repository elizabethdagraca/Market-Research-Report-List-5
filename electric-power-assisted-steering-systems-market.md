<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) systems encompass three main types: C-EPAS, P-EPAS, and R-EPAS. C-EPAS refers to column-mounted systems that offer compact designs suitable for various vehicle layouts. P-EPAS involves rack-mounted systems that provide enhanced steering feel and precision, ideal for performance vehicles. R-EPAS combines both column and rack-mounted features, delivering adaptive steering assistance based on vehicle speed and driving conditions. Together, these systems enhance vehicle handling, fuel efficiency, and overall driving comfort.</p></p>
