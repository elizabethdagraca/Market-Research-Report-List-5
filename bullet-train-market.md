<p><strong>The Bullet Train Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel on Rail</li><li>Maglev</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bullet Train market consists of two primary types: Wheel on Rail and Maglev. Wheel on Rail systems operate on traditional tracks, utilizing steel wheels to provide high-speed transportation while benefiting from established infrastructure. In contrast, Maglev trains use magnetic levitation to glide above the tracks, eliminating friction and enabling even faster speeds. Both types cater to different regional and technological demands, with Wheel on Rail being more prevalent in existing networks, while Maglev represents advanced innovations for future high-speed travel.</p></p>
