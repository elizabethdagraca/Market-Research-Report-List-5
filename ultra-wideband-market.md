<p><ul><li>Impulse Radio</li><li>Multi-band UWB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Wideband (UWB) market comprises two primary types: Impulse Radio and Multi-band UWB. Impulse Radio utilizes short-duration, low-energy pulses to transmit data, allowing for high precision in distance measurements and low power consumption. In contrast, Multi-band UWB operates by using multiple frequency bands simultaneously, enhancing data transfer rates and enabling better integration in various applications like communications, positioning, and sensing. Both types cater to industries such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics, driving innovation in wireless technologies.</p></p>
