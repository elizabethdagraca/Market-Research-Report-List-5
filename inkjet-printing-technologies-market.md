<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inkjet Printing Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scan Printing</li><li>Single-Pass Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet printing technologies encompass various market types, notably scan printing and single-pass printing. Scan printing involves moving the print head across the page, applying ink in a back-and-forth motion, which can lead to slower production speeds but allows for high-quality output. In contrast, single-pass printing employs a stationary print head that prints across the entire width of the page in one pass, enabling faster print speeds and higher efficiency, making it ideal for high-volume printing applications.</p></p>
