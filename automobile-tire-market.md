<p><p>The automobile tire market is primarily divided into two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with layers of fabric cords that run diagonally, providing a flexible and durable design, ideal for certain applications. In contrast, radial tires feature cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, resulting in better traction, improved fuel efficiency, and reduced rolling resistance. Radial tires have become more popular in recent years due to their performance advantages and longer lifespan.</p></p>
