<p><p>The GERD (Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease) and NERD (Non-Erosive Reflux Disease) treatment market consists of several key types. Antacids, or acid neutralizers, provide quick relief by neutralizing stomach acid. Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) reduce acid production by blocking the proton pumps in the stomach, offering longer-lasting relief. H2 Receptor Blockers inhibit histamine action, reducing acid secretion and providing moderate relief. Together, these treatment options cater to varying severity and duration of symptoms in patients suffering from reflux conditions.</p></p>
