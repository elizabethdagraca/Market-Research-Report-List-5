<p><p>Point heating systems encompass various technologies designed for localized heating. Electric point heating systems utilize electric resistance elements to provide direct warmth, making them efficient for small spaces. Gas infrared point heating systems generate heat through infrared radiation, ideal for outdoor or well-ventilated environments, as they heat objects and people rather than the air. Geothermal point heating systems harness geothermal energy from the Earth, offering a sustainable and energy-efficient heating solution by utilizing the consistent temperature of underground sources.</p></p>
