<p><p>The functional energy drink market is segmented into isotonic, hypertonic, hypotonic, and other types. Isotonic drinks rapidly replenish fluids and electrolytes, making them ideal for hydration during exercise. Hypertonic drinks contain higher sugar concentrations, providing quick energy but requiring additional water intake for rehydration. Hypotonic drinks have lower sugar and electrolyte content, offering moderate hydration for less intense activities. Other types may include natural or plant-based formulations, targeting specific consumer preferences for health and wellness.</p></p>
